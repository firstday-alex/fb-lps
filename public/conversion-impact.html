Top Contributors to Blended CVR Change

Each row is one utm_source. CVR Impact = this source's contribution (in pp) to the total blended CVR change for this data pull. Decomposed into: (a) own CVR effect — prevShare × ΔCvr (the source's own CVR movement, weighted by its baseline traffic share); plus (b) share-shift effect — ΔShare × (currCvr − blended avg) (gaining/losing share at a CVR above or below the blended average). Sums across all sources to the overall blended ΔCvr. Negative = dragged blended CVR down. So a below-average source whose CVR drops and gains share compounds on both terms. Dot = channel group.
